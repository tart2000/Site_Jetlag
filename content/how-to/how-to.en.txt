## Styling your post 
You can use Markdown to style your posts. 
If you're not familiar with markdown, please have a look here : 
(link: https://github.com/adam-p/markdown-here/wiki/Markdown-Cheatsheet text: https://github.com/adam-p/markdown-here/wiki/Markdown-Cheatsheet)

## Adding images 
Images can be added using the left-hand side panel. 
Once they're uploaded on the website, you can drag and drop the image where you want them in your post. 
You can also write : 
(image: image.png)
Please don't use images larger than 1800px. 

## About posts

- You can write as many posts per day as you want
- Posts have to be written in a 'day' folder
- Make sure the language of the post is set to English (even if it isn't, as it's the default language for the site)
- Please add a cover image to your 'day'
